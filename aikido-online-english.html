What is Aikido?

The Founder, Morihei Ueshiba, made Aikido 100 years ago. In Aikido we merge into the opponent, and control him/her.

We offer how to use your inner power. This doesn't need mustle power, so suited for women and children.

We offer original lessons based on traditional Aikido. You'll be cheered up after the lessons!
